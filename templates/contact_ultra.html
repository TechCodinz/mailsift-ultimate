Simply visit our homepage, paste your text or upload a file, and click extract. No registration required for basic usage!

What file formats are supported?

We support TXT, CSV, HTML, PDF, DOCX, and Excel files. Our AI can extract emails from virtually any text-based format.

How accurate is email extraction?

Our AI-powered extraction achieves 99.9% accuracy with real-time validation and duplicate removal.

Do you offer API access?

Yes! We provide a comprehensive REST API with SDKs for Python, JavaScript, and other popular languages.

We accept all major cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, USDT, etc.) and traditional credit cards for your convenience.

Yes! Our free tier includes 100 extractions per day with basic validation features.
